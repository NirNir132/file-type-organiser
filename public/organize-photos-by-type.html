Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPG/JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP, BMP, TIFF, SVG, and many others. Simply type the file extension to filter.

Can I organize photos from multiple folders at once?

Yes! Drop multiple folders simultaneously and our tool will scan all of them, including subfolders, to find your target image format.

Does the tool compress my photos?

No compression is applied. Your original photos maintain their quality, resolution, and metadata throughout the organization process.

Can I extract photos from ZIP archives?

Absolutely! Extract the ZIP contents in your browser first, then use our tool to organize the photos by file type.

Is there a limit on photo file sizes?

No limits! Organize high-resolution camera photos, large PNG graphics, or any size image files.
